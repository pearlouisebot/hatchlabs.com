About Hatch Labs

Hatch Labs Holdings LLC is a venture studio headquartered in Austin, Texas. We identify opportunities in the consumer mobile space, build products from scratch, and acquire existing apps with strong fundamentals and growth potential.

Our approach combines deep product expertise, data-driven decision making, and lean operations. We focus on categories where technology can meaningfully improve how people connect, communicate, and make decisions.

Founded by Amanda Bradford — who previously built and sold The League, a dating app acquired by Match Group — Hatch Labs brings a founder's operational mindset to every product in our portfolio.

We are a registered Delaware LLC with a physical office in Austin. We work with partners including Plaid, Apple, Google, and Amazon to bring our products to market.

What We Do

Build

We develop consumer mobile applications from concept through launch. Our current portfolio includes products in dating, social networking, and personal finance — categories where verified identity and real data create better user experiences.

Acquire

We acquire existing mobile apps with established user bases and revenue. We look for products with strong retention, clear monetization, and opportunities to improve through better technology and operations.

Scale

We apply a shared infrastructure layer across our portfolio — identity verification, payment processing, analytics, and growth marketing — to scale apps efficiently without reinventing the wheel for each product.
