About Hatch Labs

Hatch Labs Holdings LLC is a venture studio headquartered in Austin, Texas. We build consumer web and mobile applications from the ground up, and we acquire existing apps with strong fundamentals and room to grow.

Our focus areas include dating and relationships, social networking, personal finance, and health and wellness — categories where better technology and verified data create meaningfully better products.

Founded by Amanda Bradford, who previously built The League — a dating app that grew to over 2 million users across 155 cities before being acquired by Match Group — Hatch Labs brings a founder-operator's perspective to every product we touch.

We are a registered Delaware LLC with offices in Austin, TX. We partner with Plaid, Apple, Google, and Amazon to bring our products to market. Our team combines deep product expertise with data-driven decision making and lean operations.

What We Do

Three core activities drive our business.

🛠️

Build Apps

We develop consumer web and mobile applications from concept through App Store launch. Our engineering team handles iOS, Android, and web — from UX design and prototyping to backend infrastructure, API integrations, and production deployment.

🔑

Acquire Apps

We acquire existing mobile apps and SaaS products with established user bases and proven revenue. We look for strong retention metrics, clear monetization, and untapped potential that our operational playbook can unlock.

📈

Scale Products

We apply a shared infrastructure layer across our portfolio — identity verification, payment processing, analytics, growth marketing, and compliance — to scale each product efficiently without duplicating effort.

How We Work

Our process for taking products from zero to market.

Identify

We analyze market gaps in the consumer space using proprietary data, user research, and competitive intelligence.

Validate

We test concepts rapidly with real users — landing pages, prototypes, and beta cohorts before committing to full development.

Build

Our engineering team ships production-quality iOS, Android, and web apps using modern frameworks and our shared infrastructure layer.

Scale

We optimize for retention, monetization, and growth. Every product benefits from our portfolio-wide analytics and marketing engine.
