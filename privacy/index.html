5. Data Retention and Deletion

5.1 Retention Periods

Active Account Data:
We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active and you continue to use our services.

Financial Verification Data:
We retain financial verification data for a minimum period required by financial service regulations, typically:

  • Identity verification records: 5 years after account closure
  • Income verification records: 3 years after account closure
  • Anti-fraud data: 7 years after account closure

5.2 Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time by contacting us at amanda@hatchlabs.com. Upon account deletion:

  • Profile information deleted within 30 days
  • Financial verification data deleted within 30 days (subject to legal requirements)
  • Messages and communications deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymous usage analytics may be retained

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to request information about what personal data we have collected about you, including categories of information, sources, and purposes for collection.

6.2 California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the CCPA:

  • Right to Know: Request specific information about how we collect, use, and share your personal information
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of the sale of your personal information (Note: We do not sell personal information)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your rights

7. Security Measures

7.1 Technical Safeguards

Data Encryption:

  • All data encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption via Supabase
  • All data in transit protected with TLS 1.2 or higher encryption
  • Database-level row security to limit data access
  • Encryption key management through secure cloud infrastructure

Access Controls:

  • Multi-factor authentication required for all critical system access
  • Role-based access control with principle of least privilege
  • Regular access reviews and immediate deactivation upon termination
  • Administrative access logging and monitoring
